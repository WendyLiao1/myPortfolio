 Uncertainty is the term that ties all my significant influences together. It is composed of the five themes in my significant influences: exploration, process, changing and adapting mindset, discovery, and storytelling. Everything is built out of uncertainty until it evolves into a finding, a solution, an idea, and a developed piece. Looking back, I am always someone who is constantly changing and moving forward for challenges even if I have chances of failing it. It turns out every choice has a meaning to me, and it lead to differnt pathway that you will never know what is going on. Through my experience, I created two posters that speak toward this idea and who I am.
